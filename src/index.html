About Rogers Park

Rogers Park is a vibrant and diverse neighborhood located on the far north side of Chicago, Illinois. Known for its cultural diversity, great food scene, proximity to Lake Michigan, and being home to Loyola University Chicago.

Rogers Park is my current home, and I really love living here so much! There is so much to do and I really love the community. I enjoy my home so much that I decided to make this website! :)

How to get there?!?

Now you have seen some of the great things that Rogers Park has to offer. So naturally you want to try and visit as soon as possible! Luckily for you, there are many great transportation options available.

By Train

The CTA Red Line has several stops in Rogers Park, including Howard, Jarvis, Morse, and Loyola. The Red Line provides easy access to downtown Chicago and other parts of the city. Additionally, the Purple and Yellow Lines have stops at Howard.

By Bus

Several CTA bus routes serve Rogers Park, including the 22 Clark, 155 Devon, 96 Lunt, and 147 Lake Shore Express just to name a few. These buses provide easy connectionsto other parts of the city that the trains do not reach.

By Car

If you prefer to drive, Rogers Park can accommodate you too. The neighborhood is located near several major roads, including the Edens Expressway and Lake Shore Drive. However, parking can be limited in some areas, so be sure to plan ahead for finding parking!
